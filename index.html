Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026  |  Application: Youshi – Amazon Seller Analytics Integration

1. Overview

Youshi ("we", "us", or "our") operates an internal business analytics platform that integrates with Amazon Seller Central via the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) and Login with Amazon (LWA). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data obtained through these integrations.

2. Data We Access

Through the Amazon SP-API, we access the following data solely to provide internal analytics and operations management:

  • Advertising campaign reports (spend, impressions, clicks, sales)
  • Order and sales reports
  • Inventory and catalog data
  • Seller account profile information

3. How We Use the Data

  • Internal business intelligence dashboards and reporting
  • Advertising performance analysis and optimization
  • Inventory and supply-chain management
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third parties

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on privately hosted infrastructure accessible only to authorized personnel. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access control, and regular security audits.

5. Data Retention

Amazon Seller data is retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes. Data is deleted upon request or when access authorization is revoked.
